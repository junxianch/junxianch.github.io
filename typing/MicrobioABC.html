    Sonnet XXI

    Soneet XXV

A is for amoeba.

All living things are made of cells. Amoebas are living organisms made of just one cell!

We are living organisms made of many cells.

B is for b-cell.

b-cells are made in our bones and help to fight infections.

c is for chloroplast.

Chloroplast are found in plant cells. They transform sunlight into energy for the cell.

D is for DNA.

DNA is like a blueprint for the cell; It provides instructions for how to build proteins.

E is for E. coli.

e. coli is a bacteria that can make us sick with diarrhea.

F is for flagella.

A flagella is like a tail for cells.

G is for Golgi.

The Golgi help and transport proteins within a cell.

H is for h. pylori.

H. pylori is a bacteria that can gives us a bad stomachache.

I is for influenza.

Influenza in the name of the virus that causes the flu.

J is for cell junction.

Cell junctions help cells stay together and communicate. It is like a bridge between two cells.

K is for natural killer cell.

Natural killer cells destroy cancer cells as well as cells infected with viruses.

L is for lysosome.

A lysosome is like the cell’s stomach. Lysosomes can even “vomit” waste out of the cell.

M is for mitochondria.

Mitochondria are parts of the cell that make energy.

N is for cell nucleus. The cell nucleus holds and Protects a cell’s DNA. O is for organelles. Specialized cell parts are called Organelles. For example, the Nucleus and Golgi are organelles. P is for plasma membrane. The plasma membrane of a cell is like Its skin; in holds the cell together and Protects it from bacteria and viruses. Q is for q fever Q fever is a disease caused by this Bacteria, which can actually hide Inside some of our cells. R is for rods and cones. Light rods cones Rods and cones are special cell types in The eye. They detect light and send A signal to the brain for us to see. S is for… Smooth endoplasmic reticulum. The smooth endoplasmic reticulum is an Organelle that manufactures many Compounds and makes poisonous Chemicals safer for the cell. T is for t-cell. t-cell help keep us safe by fighting bad cells and viruses. T-cells are made in the part of the body called the thymus. U is for unicellular. Unicellular means made of just one cell (like this paramecium), whereas multicellular Means made of two of more cells (like us). V is for virus Viruses are small microscopic Structures that infect other cells. This virus illustrated infects bacteria. W is for cell wall. Plant cells and have an Extra layer of protection around Them called a cell wall. X is for Xylem. Xylems are found inside plants And they help to move water And nutrients around. Y is for yeast. Yeast is a type of unicellular animal cell. There are many different types of yeast. Some help our body, others hurt it. Still others we use in making bread! Z is for zooplankton. Zooplankton, such as these rotifers, are Tiny microscopic animals that live in water. Without these microscopic critters, marine Life in the oceans would not possible. Now you Know your Microbiology Abc’s
