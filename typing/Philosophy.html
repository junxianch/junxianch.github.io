    Soneet XXV

From Children's Book of Philosophy

1. Heraclitus famously remarked that you cannot step twice into the same river.

2. Funny story about Thales was so busy with wondering what was happenning in heaven that he did not see where he was going and fell down a well.

3. Scientists say that the universe started with a Big Bang ---- a powerful explosion.

4. Anaximander of miletus agreed with Thales that everything in the world developed from one substance, but it made four basic elements of earth, water, air, and fire.

5. Empedocles thought the elements were controlled by two powerful forces: love and strife, or disagreement.

6. Socrates said: I cannot teach anybody anything, I can only make them think.

7. Plato said: the object of education is to teach us to love what is beautiful.

8. Aristotle said: the roots of education are bitter, but the fruit is sweet.

9. The ship of Theseus: Theseus is born in the village of Chearonea, after he dies he dies in Delphi, Greece,

10. Rene Descartes said: it is not enough to have a good mind; the main thing is to use it well.

11. John Loke said: where there is no law, there is no freedom.

12. Siddhartha Gautama said: there is no law that is permanent and unchangeable.

13.Immanuel Kant said: human reason troubled by questions that it cannot dismiss, but also cannot answer.

14. Simone de Beauvoir said: be loved, be admired, be necessary; be somebody.
